OUR PRIVACY POLICY

UPDATED: JUNE 2025

We’re parents too, we get it.

You’re trusting us with your information, and that’s a big deal. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we keep it safe across our website, waitlist, and the Tasky app.

1. Who We Are

We’re Tasky, a playful app made for kids and parents, helping you track habits and reward good behaviour. Our website is thetaskyapp.com and our app is available via iOS/Android (coming soon!).

2. What We Collect

We only collect what we need to make Tasky useful for you:

On the Website & Waitlist:

  • Your name and email address (if you sign up)
  • Device info and usage data (like most websites, we use analytics to understand how people use the site)

In the Tasky App (once live):

  • Your name, email and location (whilst using the app)
  • App usage data (to improve the experience)
  • Optional submissions (if you suggest an activity or leave feedback)

We do not collect any sensitive information like health data or financial info.

3. How We Use Your Info

We use your info to:

  • Help you track habits and tasks
  • Personalise your experience
  • Let you know when Tasky launches new features
  • Improve the app and fix bugs
  • Send occasional updates (only if you’ve said yes)

We do not sell your data. Ever.

4. Cookies and Tracking

Like most websites, we use cookies and analytics tools (like Google Analytics) to see what pages are popular and how people find us. You can turn cookies off in your browser settings any time.

5. Sharing Your Data

We only share your data when:

  • It’s required to run the service (e.g. with our email provider or developers)
  • It’s required by law

We don’t share your personal info with advertisers or unrelated third parties.

6. Keeping Your Info Safe

We store your data securely and follow industry best practices to protect it. If there’s ever a data breach, we’ll let you know.

7. Your Rights

You can:

  • Ask us what info we have on you
  • Ask us to delete your data
  • Opt out of emails at any time

Just email us at support@thetaskyapp.com and we’ll help.

8. Changes to This Policy

We might update this privacy policy if things change. We’ll post updates on this page and let users know if it’s a big change.

9. Contact Us

Got a question? You can reach us at:

  • Email: hello@thetaskyapp.com
  • Or via our contact form
